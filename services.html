Communication with Dance Studios and Teachers

Ongoing communication with dancer's teachers allows for better incorporation of physiotherapy principles into their training.

Flexibility Workshops

Workshops focusing on safely increasing flexibility by targeting the nervous, fascial, and musculoskeletal systems.
Designed for dancers age 10 and up. Offered in studio. Maximum 15 dancers.

Extensions Workshops

Workshops focusing on increasing the height of dancer's leg extensions to the front, side, and back.
Designed for Intermediate and Advanced dancers. Offered in studio. Maximum 15 dancers.

Dance Conditioning Workshops

Workshops focusing on overall strength and conditioning, incorporating the principles into their technical training.
Designed for dancers age 10 and up. Offered in studio. Maximum 15 dancers.
