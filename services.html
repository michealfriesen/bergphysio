Services Offered

Physiotherapy Assessments aim to assess and diagnose acute and/or chronic injuries. This is done using various methods including: orthopedic "special tests", functional movement assessments, range of motion measurements, strength measurements, and more!

Physiotherapy Treatments offered include: IMS/dry needling, myofascial release, exercise prescription, mobility training, kinesiology taping, cupping, and more!

Dance Physiotherapy Assessments can be used to assess and diagnose current injuries, or to identify injury prevention strategies. This includes assessments of technique, movement patterns, range of motion, flexibility, and strength.

Flexibility Workshops

Workshops focusing on safely increasing flexibility by targeting the nervous, fascial, and musculoskeletal systems.
Designed for dancers age 10 and up. Offered in studio. Maximum 15 dancers.

Extensions Workshops

Workshops focusing on increasing the height of dancer's leg extensions to the front, side, and back.
Designed for Intermediate and Advanced dancers. Offered in studio. Maximum 15 dancers.

Dance Conditioning Workshops

Workshops focusing on overall strength and conditioning, incorporating the principles into their technical training.
Designed for dancers age 10 and up. Offered in studio. Maximum 15 dancers.

Injury Prevention Workshops

This research-based workshop explores the common causes of dancer's injuries and the current findings about injury prevention.
Can be altered for a dancer-based workshop, or a teacher-based workshop. Offered in studio. Maximum 15 dancers.
