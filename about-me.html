About Rachelle

Education

Rachelle completed a Masters of Science in Physical Therapy at University of Alberta in 2020, and is a registered Physiotherapist with the College of Physiotherapists of Alberta. She has continued to prioritize learning and growing within her career.

Rachelle is a certified IMS/Dry Needling Practitioner.

Dance Experience and Physiotherapy

Rachelle started dancing at Crossings when she was 5 years old. She was a member of Corps Bara Youth Dance Company for 7 years. Rachelle completed her Advanced I RAD Ballet Exam and her Advanced I ISTD Modern Exam. She continued dancing with Crossings until 2021, resulting in over 20 years of dance experience!

Combining her love of dance and physiotherapy has long been her passion. Providing physiotherapy to dancers is the culmination of many years of dancing, education and dreaming.

General Physiotherapy

Rachelle is passionate about helping people recover from their injuries and achieve their movement goals. She believes in active treatment and the importance of exercise and strengthening in recovery.

Rachelle also focuses on preventative and proactive treatment. You don't have to be injured to benefit from physiotherapy!
